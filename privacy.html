BlueJay - Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-01-01

Contact: support@bluejaytuning.com

What we collect.When you install or use BlueJay, we may collect: license key, device/app identifiers (e.g., hardware hash, OS and app version), IP address, and information you choose to provide to support.

Why we collect it.We use this information to:

  • activate and verify your license;
  • prevent fraud and abuse;
  • provide customer support and product updates.

Sharing. We don’t sell personal data. We share it only with service providers we use to host, store, or process licensing data, under contract, and when required by law.

Retention. We keep license records for as long as your license is active and for 5 years afterward for audit and compliance, then delete or anonymize them.

Your choices & rights. You can contact us at support@bluejaytuning.com to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. Depending on your location, you may have additional rights (e.g., under GDPR/CCPA).

Security. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information (e.g., TLS in transit). No method is 100% secure.

Children. Our app is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region), and we don’t knowingly collect data from them.

Changes. We may update this policy. We’ll post the new date above and, if changes are material, provide additional notice.
